## Decision
Difficulty will progress by increasing the speed of the world and the frequency of obstacles over time. The speed will increase by 10% every 30 seconds, and obstacle frequency will increase by 5% every 20 seconds.

## Rationale
- Provides a clear and explainable difficulty progression.
- Ensures that the game remains challenging and engaging as the player progresses.
